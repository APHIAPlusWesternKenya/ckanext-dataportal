    ILRI's Open Data Policy

    ILRI regards the results of its research and development activities as international public goods and is committed to their widespread dissemination and use to achieve the maximum impact to advantage the poor, especially smallholder farmers in developing countries. ILRI considers open access as a practical application of this principle.

    ILRI as a CGIAR centre complies with the broad CGIAR Open Access Policy.
